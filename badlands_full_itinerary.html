🏞️ Full Badlands Family Road Trip Itinerary

📅 Day 1: Tuesday, May 19 – Louisville, KY → Davenport, IA

  • 8:00 AM – Depart Louisville
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch in Bloomington, IL
  • 2:00 PM – Arrive in Davenport, IA
  • 3:00 PM – Check-in at Fairfield Inn & Suites Davenport
  • 3:30 PM – Explore Vander Veer Botanical Park
  • 5:30 PM – Dinner at Grapevine Bar & Grill or Barrel House 211

📅 Day 2: Wednesday, May 20 – Davenport, IA → Sioux Falls, SD

  • 8:30 AM – Depart Davenport
  • 1:00 PM – Lunch in Sioux City
  • 3:00 PM – Arrive in Sioux Falls, SD
  • 3:30 PM – Visit Butterfly House & Aquarium
  • 6:00 PM – Dinner at Carnaval Brazilian Grill
  • 7:30 PM – Check-in at Residence Inn Sioux Falls

📅 Day 3: Thursday, May 21 – Sioux Falls, SD → Rapid City, SD

  • 9:00 AM – Depart Sioux Falls
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch in Murdo
  • 2:00 PM – Arrive in Rapid City
  • 3:00 PM – Check-in at Fairfield Inn & Suites Rapid City
  • 3:30 PM – Water fun at WaTiki Indoor Waterpark
  • 6:00 PM – Dinner at Dakotah Steakhouse or Que Pasa

📅 Day 4: Friday, May 22 – Badlands National Park

  • 8:30 AM – Depart for Badlands
  • 9:30 AM – Scenic drive: Door Trail, Notch Trail, Big Badlands Overlook
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch at Wall Drug Café
  • 4:00 PM – Return to hotel
  • Evening – Relax or visit WaTiki again

📅 Day 5: Saturday, May 23 – Mt. Rushmore & Custer State Park

  • 8:30 AM – Depart for Mt. Rushmore
  • 9:30 AM – Explore monument, ice cream, Jr Ranger program
  • 11:30 AM – Scenic drive to Custer State Park
  • 12:30 PM – Picnic lunch on Wildlife Loop
  • 3:30 PM – Return to hotel
  • 6:00 PM – Dinner (pizza or quick service)

📅 Day 6: Sunday, May 24 – Rapid City → Omaha, NE

  • 8:00 AM – Depart Rapid City
  • 1:00 PM – Lunch stop in North Platte or Ogallala
  • 3:30 PM – Visit Henry Doorly Zoo & Aquarium
  • 6:00 PM – Check-in at Residence Inn Omaha Downtown

📅 Day 7: Monday, May 25 – Omaha → Columbia, MO

  • 9:00 AM – Depart Omaha
  • 12:30 PM – Lunch in Kansas City
  • 3:30 PM – Visit Runge Nature Center
  • 5:00 PM – Check-in at SpringHill Suites Columbia
  • 6:30 PM – Dinner at Flat Branch Pub

📅 Day 8: Tuesday, May 26 – Columbia → Louisville, KY

  • 9:00 AM – Depart Columbia
  • 12:00 PM – Lunch in Mt. Vernon, IL or Evansville, IN
  • 3:00 PM – Arrive home

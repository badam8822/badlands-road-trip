Badlands Family Road Trip — Full Itinerary

Tuesday, May 19 – Louisville, KY → Davenport, IA

  • Depart: 8:00 AM
  • Arrive: 2:00 PM (with 1-hour lunch in Bloomington, IL)
  • Hotel: Fairfield Inn & Suites Davenport Quad Cities
  • Activity: Vander Veer Botanical Park (3:30–5:00 PM)
  • Dinner: Grapevine Bar & Grill or Barrel House 211 (5:30–6:30 PM)

Wednesday, May 20 – Davenport, IA → Sioux Falls, SD

  • Depart: 8:30 AM
  • Arrive: 3:00 PM (with 1-hour break in Sioux City)
  • Hotel: Residence Inn Sioux Falls
  • Activity: Butterfly House & Aquarium (3:30–5:30 PM)
  • Dinner: Carnaval Brazilian Grill (6:00–7:00 PM)

Thursday, May 21 – Sioux Falls, SD → Rapid City, SD

  • Depart: 9:00 AM
  • Arrive: 2:00 PM (with lunch in Murdo)
  • Hotel: Fairfield Inn & Suites Rapid City (3 nights)
  • Activity: WaTiki Indoor Waterpark (3:00–5:30 PM)
  • Dinner: Dakotah Steakhouse or Que Pasa Cantina

Friday, May 22 – Badlands National Park

  • Depart: 8:30 AM
  • Badlands Loop: 9:30 AM – 1:00 PM (Door Trail, Notch Trail, Big Badlands Overlook)
  • Junior Ranger program at Visitor Center
  • Lunch in Wall, SD: 1:30–2:30 PM (Wall Drug Café)
  • Optional Stop: Prairie Dog Ranch
  • Return to Hotel: ~4:00 PM
  • Evening at Waterpark or rest

Saturday, May 23 – Mount Rushmore & Custer State Park

  • Depart: 8:30 AM
  • Mt. Rushmore: 9:30–11:00 AM (Junior Ranger, ice cream, walk)
  • Drive to Custer State Park: 11:00–12:00 PM
  • Wildlife Loop Picnic Lunch: 12:00–1:30 PM
  • Return to Hotel: ~3:30 PM
  • Dinner: Pizza or quick-service near hotel
  • Optional: Evening Waterpark Visit

Sunday, May 24 – Rapid City, SD → Omaha, NE

  • Depart: 8:00 AM
  • Lunch Stop: North Platte or Ogallala
  • Hotel: Residence Inn Omaha Downtown/Old Market
  • Activity: Henry Doorly Zoo & Aquarium (3:30–6:00 PM)

Monday, May 25 – Omaha, NE → Columbia, MO

  • Depart: 9:00 AM
  • Lunch Stop: Kansas City
  • Hotel: SpringHill Suites Columbia
  • Activity: Runge Nature Center (3:30–5:00 PM)
  • Dinner: Flat Branch Pub

Tuesday, May 26 – Columbia, MO → Louisville, KY

  • Depart: 9:00 AM
  • Lunch Stop: Mt. Vernon, IL or Evansville, IN
  • Arrive Home: ~3:00 PM
